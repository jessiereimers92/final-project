Restaurants

Madrid, and Spain in general, has awesome food. However, their specialty is tapas and there are hundreds of tapas places all over the city. This list is a list of the restaurants my friends, family and I went to while we were in Madrid.

  1. Lamucca
    • Location: Metro stop Sol, Calle del Prado 16
    • Website
  2. Lateral
    • A really nice tapas place
    • Location: Six in total in the city. Visit their website to find them all.
  3. Mercado de San Miguel
    • A food market with all different types of food
    • Location: Metro stop Sol, Plaza San Miguel
    • Website
  4. El Tigre
    • Tapas bar and restaurant
    • Six euros for a large drink (mojito, sangria, beer, or tinto verano) and a plate of tapas
    • Great for dinner or just for a bite to eat before going out for the night
    • Locations: Metro stop Gran Vía, Calle Hortaleza 32 or Calle de las Infantas 28
    • Yelp page
  5. Cien Montaditos
    • Cheap, but good, little sandwhiches with a hundred different options for what you want on your sandwhich.
    • Tip: On Wednesdays, everything is half price
    • Location: Scattered throughout the city. Visit their website to find all their locations.
  6. Tierra
    • Burrito place that is like Chipotle
    • Locations: Metro stop Islas Filipinas: Calle Guzman el Bueno 52 and metro stop Alonso Martinez: Calle Sagasta 30
    • Website
  7. Chocolatería San Ginés
    • Famous for its churros con chocolate
    • Location: Metro stop Sol, Pasadizo San Ginés 5
    • Website
