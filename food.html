Restaurants

  1. Lamucca
    • Metro stop: Sol
    • Calle del Prado 16
    • Website
  2. Lateral
    • A really nice tapas place
    • Metro stop:Sol
    • Plaza Santa Ana
    • Website
  3. El Tigre
    • Tapas bar and restaurant
    • Six euros for a large drink (mojito, sangria, beer, or tinto verano) and a plate of tapas
    • Great for dinner or just for a bite to eat before going out for the night
    • Locations: Calle Hortaleza 32 or Calle de las Infantas 28
    • Yelp page
  5. Cien Montaditos
    • Cheap, but good, little sandwhiches with a hundred different options for what you want on your sandwhich.
    • Tip: On Wednesdays, everything is half price
    • Location: Scattered throughout the city. Visit their website to find all their locations.
