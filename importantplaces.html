Important Places to Visit

Madrid is a beautiful city with great museums and historical sites. This is a list of the most important sites to see.

  1. Templo de Debod
    • An ancient Egyptian temple rebuilt in Madrid
    • It's best to go at sunset because it's so beautiful
    • Location: Metro stop Ventura Rodriguez, Calle Ferraz 1
  2. Reina Sofía
    • Spain's modern art museum
    • Pablo Picasso's "Guernica" is at this museum
    • Location: Metro stop Atocha, Calle de Santa Isabel 52
    • Website
  3. El Prado
    • Spain's national art museum
    • Location: Metro stop Atocha, Paseo del Prado
    • Website
  4. Parque del Retiro
    • The Central Park of Madrid
    • Location: Metro stop Retiro or Ibiza, Plaza de la Independencia 7
    • Website
  5. Palacio de Cristal
    • A house made totally of glass and metal
    • Location: Metro stop Retiro, Paseo República de Cuba 4 (inside the park)
    • Website
  6. El Rastro
    • Flea market that takes place every Sunday morning. It's best to get there early before it gets too crowded
    • Location: Metro stop La Latina
    • Google+ page
  7. Plaza Mayor
    • A nice place to relax and get café con leche
    • Location: Metro stop Sol
    • Google+ page
  8. Palacio Real
    • The Royal Palace of Madrid
    • Location: Metro stop Opera, Calle Bailén
    • Website
  9. Catedral de la Almudena
    • The Catholic cathedral in Madrid
    • Location: Metro stop Opera, Calle Bailén 10
    • Website
  10. Corral de la Morería
    • Spain is famous for flamenco and this is a great place to see a show
    • You can buy a ticket for the show with dinner and drinks or just drinks or just dinner
    • Location: Metro stop La Latina, Calle de la Morería 17
    • Website
  11. Palacio de Cibeles
    • If you go inside and climb to the top, you'll have a great view of the city
    • Location: Metro stop Banco de España, Plaza de Cibeles
  12. Plaza de Toros de la Ventas
    • Spain is famous for bullfighting and Plaza de Toros is where they happen
    • While I didn't like the bullfight, it was definitely worth it to see a bullfight and to see that aspect of Spanish culture
    • Location: Metro stop Ventas, Calle de Alcalá 237
    • Website
