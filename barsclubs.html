Bars and Clubs

Madrid is know for their nightlife. People go out around midnight and don't go home until around four or five AM. This is a list of the bars and clubs my friends and I went to while we were in Madrid.

Bars

There are bars all over the city but a big number of the good ones are in Sol. Some of them are a little touristy, but still fun.

  1. La Fontana de Oro
    • Location: Metro stop Sol, Calle de la Victoria 1
    • Website
  2. Dubliners
    • An Irish pub that a lot of American students go to
    • They have a great deal of a beer and a shot for only 4 euros
    • Location: Metro stop Sol, Calle de Espoz y Mina 7
    • Website
  3. O'Connell, S.T.
    • Another Irish pub right next to Dubliners
    • Location: Metro stop Sol, Calle de Espoz y Mina 7
    • Website
  4. La Chapandaz

Clubs

  1. Kapital
    • This is a must. It is a seven story discotecta
    • The cover is a little pricey but totally worth it
    • Location: Metro stop Atocha, Calle de Atocha 25
    • Website
  2. Joy
    • Thursday nights has really good music
    • Smaller than Kapital but still fun
    • Location: Metro stop Opera or Sol, Calle del Arenal 11
    • Website
  3. Moondance
    • Fun mix between a bar and club
    • Location: Metro stop Sevilla or Sol, Calle Aduana 21
